Our Church Kids

Birth through 5th Grade

We believe church should be fun! Every Sunday, your children will learn about Jesus in a safe, age-appropriate environment. Our background-checked volunteers are passionate about helping kids build a foundation of faith that lasts a lifetime.

Serving God

Church Volunteers

Our volunteers faithfully serve God by using their gifts to strengthen the church and support its ministries. From worship leadership and event planning to evangelism and outreach, volunteers help create opportunities for spiritual growth, fellowship, and sharing the Gospel. Together, we serve with one purpose: to glorify God and impact lives for Christ.

Our Worship Team

Praise & Worship

We use our gifts and talents to honor God and lead others into worship. Our team is committed to spiritual growth, excellence, and serving the church body. Whether you're a vocalist, instrumentalist, or interested in learning, we invite you to grow with us as we worship and serve God together.

Our worship team dedicates time each week to glorify God through music and praise. We meet every Sunday morning for vocal warm-ups, sound check, and rehearsal before the English service. New members are always welcome.
