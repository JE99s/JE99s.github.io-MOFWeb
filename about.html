Our Story.

Building a community where faith meets real life.

We exist to help you find your purpose.

Mission of Faith was established in 1969 with a simple goal: to create a place where everyone is welcome, regardless of their background or past. What started as a small gathering has grown into a vibrant family dedicated to serving Rosenberg and the surrounding areas.

We believe that church isn't just a building you walk into on Sunday, but a community you are a part of every day of the week.

Meet Our Lead Pastors.

What We Believe.

In essentials, unity; in non-essentials, liberty; in all things, charity.

The Bible

We believe the entire Bible is the inspired Word of God. It is without error and is our ultimate guide for what to believe and how to live.

God

We believe in one God who exists eternally in three distinct persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. He is the creator of all things.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus is the Son of God. He lived a sinless life, died on the cross for our sins, and rose again so we can have eternal life.

Salvation

We believe salvation is a free gift from God. It cannot be earned through good works; it is received by faith in Jesus Christ.

The Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ on earth, empowered by the Holy Spirit to share the hope of Jesus with the world.
